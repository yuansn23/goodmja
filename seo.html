What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization for Business Growth

More Than Technology, It's a Growth Engine

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is a promotional technique that utilizes search engine ranking algorithms to improve a website's natural ranking in search results (non-advertising) through various optimization techniques.

When potential customers develop purchasing needs and search for related products or businesses on Google, the business website appears in the natural search results, enhancing the business's authority in the customer's mind, while also receiving clicks and visits from potential customers, significantly improving business conversion rates and brand awareness.
