Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our advertising services

I'm new to advertising. Can you provide management services?

Absolutely! From designing promotion processes and account setup to placement management, performance evaluation, and ongoing optimization, you'll receive comprehensive service. Our experienced account managers and powerful data network will precisely target your ideal customers, leaving you worry-free. You'll simply need to review the daily data reports.

Will you provide detailed advertising data reports?

Yes. For our managed services, we provide daily reports showing the previous day's advertising performance. Every day's data is meticulously recorded and shared with you, providing solid data support for your decision-making process.

Which platforms do you support for advertising?

We provide comprehensive advertising services across major platforms including Facebook, Instagram, Google, YouTube, TikTok, and Kwai. Our team specializes in creating cross-platform strategies that amplify your message and maximize your return on ad spend.

How long does it take to see results from advertising campaigns?

While initial results can often be seen within the first week, meaningful performance data typically requires 2-3 weeks as the campaigns optimize. We continuously adjust strategies based on real-time performance data. Most clients see significant improvements in their key metrics within the first month of our partnership.
